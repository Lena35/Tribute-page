Ashton Kutcher

Known from That 70's Show

Ashton Kutcher, a famous actor, well known from That 70's Show. Also plays in Two and a Half Man and several popular action movies and romance movies. He also invested in a large number of companies and start-ups.

Here's a time line of Ashton Kutchers life:

  • 1978 - Born in Cedar Rapids, Iowa
  • 1996 - Enrolls at the University of Iowa
  • 1998 - Starts a successful modelling career
  • 1998 - Is cast as Michael Kelso in That 70's Show
  • 2000 - Starts his movie actor career
  • 2003 - Produces and hosts his own tv reality show
  • 2005 - Marries Demi Moore
  • 2011 - Is cast as Walden Schmidt in Two and a Half Men
  • 2013 - Divorces Demi Moore
  • 2014 - Becomes engaged to Mila Kunis, who gives birth to their daughter Isabelle soon after
  • 2015 - Marries Mila Kunis
  • 2016 - His son Dimitri is born
